ORIGAMI DESIGNS

* INTERSTING FACTS ABOUT CAMEL

* Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
* Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can’t eat.
* Camels can completely shut their nostrils during sandstorms.
* Thanks to thick pads of skin on their chest and knees, camels can comfortably sit in very hot sand.


* INTERSTING FACTS ABOUT CHAMELEON

* They mainly change color in order to communicate or regulate body temperature.
* Chameleons have eyes in the backs of their heads.
* The tongue of a chameleon can be longer than its own body length.
* Chameleon spit is unbelievably sticky.


* INTERSTING FACTS ABOUT PIGEON

* Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
* Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities.
* Pigeons can fly at altitudes up to and beyond 6000 feet, and at an average speed of 77.6 mph.
*Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities.


* INTERSTING FACTS ABOUT PANDA

* Giant pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim.
* Pandas have carnivorous teeth, but they eat bamboo and fruit.
* A panda cannot hibernate due to the lack of fat reserve from its bamboo diet.
* Pandas live longer in captivity than in the wild.


* INTERSTING FACTS ABOUT CICADA

* Their wings are transparent and can seem rainbow-hued when held up to a light source.
* The veins on the tips of the wings of some cicadas make the shape of a W.
* Cicadas which are also known as ‘TINY VIOLINS’.
* Cicadas make noise like toy frogs.
